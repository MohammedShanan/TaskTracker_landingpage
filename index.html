About TaskTracker

TaskTracker was inspired by my own need for a more efficient and user-friendly way to manage tasks and projects. After struggling with existing tools that felt overly complex or lacked the right features, I decided to create a solution that prioritizes simplicity and usability. The project began as a personal challenge to improve my skills in web development and has grown into a tool I am proud to share with others. This project is also part of my portfolio for [ALX](https://www.alxafrica.com/), where I am honing my skills in software engineering.
